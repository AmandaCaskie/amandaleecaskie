Jennie Robinson Faber

I’m a front-end Web developer and product designer, with a background in editing and content strategy. I am a partner in Bento Box Projects, Inc., a Web and application development company based in Toronto.

I co-founded Bento Miso Collaborative Workspace, a work + play space and community for creative humans of all kinds (but especially independent game developers and artists), and Dames Making Games, a not-for-profit organization dedicated to supporting women, nonbinary and genderqueer gamemakers. I’m also on the board of the Toronto Media Arts Centre, a major arts facility set to open in Toronto’s Queen West neighborhood very soon.

You can find me on Twitter, Instagram, Flickr and Github. In my spare time, I enjoy helping out with open-source software projects such as Daimio and iV.

Feel free to any time.
