Jennie Robinson Faber

I’m a developer focused on the front end and inclusive workflows, a community facilitator and advocate for equity in visual arts & culture.

For 15 years, I’ve honed my unique combination of design, editorial and technical skills on hundreds of widely varying client projects. My primary interest is in building tools and infrastructure for creative people to do their thing. HTML, CSS and JavaScript are my bread and butter.

I’m on the board of the Toronto Media Arts Centre, a major arts + tech facility set to open in Toronto’s Queen West neighborhood in 2017. I also co-founded Gamma Space Collaborative Studio, a work + play space and community for creative humans of all kinds (but especially independent game developers and artists), and Dames Making Games, a not-for-profit organization dedicated to supporting women, nonbinary and gender non-conforming gamemakers.

You can find me on Twitter, Instagram, Flickr and Github. In my spare time, I enjoy helping out with open-source software projects such as Daimio and iV.

I’m always open to chatting about potential collaborations. Drop me a line!
