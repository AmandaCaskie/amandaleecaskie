Toronto Media Arts Centre

I’m on the board of the Toronto Media Arts Centre, a purpose-built media arts facility set to open in Toronto’s Queen West neighborhood in 2017.

Dames Making Games

I co-founded and am the executive director of Dames Making Games, a not-for-profit videogame arts organization dedicated to supporting women, nonbinary, femme, and gender non-conforming gamemakers.

Gamma Space Collaborative Studio

I am a founder and director of Gamma Space Collaborative Studio, a shared workspace, event venue and community for creatives – especially independent game designers, programmers and artists working on the web and in VR, video, games and other screen-based media.

Bit Bazaar

I run a unique bi-annual zines-and-indie-games festival, which has featured over 200 exhibitors, 7,000+ attendees and many tacos. In addition to vendors, each festival features an exhibit of curated and commissioned original interactive works.
